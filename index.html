What you need to know

1. Acupuncture NZ members only use pre-sterilized use needles.

2. Usually no more than 6-10 needles are used in each treatment.

3. The needles stay in place for about 20 minutes.

4. There is no age limit for treatment - everyone from babies to the elderly may benefit.

5. Advise your practitioner if you are pregnant - some points are recommended during pregnancy.

6. Chinese herbs may be in raw form or offered as a freeze-dried powder, pills or capsules.

7. Acupuncture NZ members are recognised ACC treatment providers.

8. Most people are surprised at how painless acupuncture can be.
