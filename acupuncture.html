What is acupuncture?

Acupuncture is a ancient Chinese practice used for medicinal purposes. The practice aims to aid in the relief of pain and help with the healing of a wide range of medical conditions. Often used in conjunction with other Chinese medical practices such as, moxibustion and cupping therapy, it is up to the practioner to diagnose the cause of the patients ailement and to decide the best course of treatment. The most common form involves inserting a number of very fine needles into specific points along a system of energy pathyways or meridians, because acupuncture works on the whole body, the acupuncturist endeavours to restore balance on every level. Often, change can not only be generated physically, but emotionally as well.
